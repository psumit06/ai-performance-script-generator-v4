My practical recommendation:

Default production model: openai/gpt-4o-mini
Fallback/escalation model: openai/gpt-4.1-mini
Avoid for normal runs: openai/gpt-4.1, unless quality failures are frequent
For your current app, gpt-4o-mini should usually be enough because the model is only classifying traffic, grouping endpoints, and producing small JSON decisions. The deterministic code builds the JMX, which keeps the model from doing the expensive, error-prone part. Use gpt-4.1-mini only for self-healing or difficult correlation cases if gpt-4o-mini gives weak repairs.

Cost-wise, your sample runs on gpt-4o-mini are roughly fractions of a cent: around $0.0002-$0.0008 per generated script before self-healing. Even a 50-endpoint enterprise capture would likely stay around a few thousandths of a dollar per run, unless retries and long HAR response previews are involved.
